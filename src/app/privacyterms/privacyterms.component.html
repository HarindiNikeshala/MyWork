Transport Service Provider is a partnership programme with small and medium businesses in Canada, to take over their transport requirements as a reliable logistic provider. By using the existing fleet, we will undertake the transport on behalf of the e-commerce merchants to ease their mind by removing the responsibility of handling the complexities of transport. The Last-mile partnership will allow the entrepreneurs to focus more on their core business products and services and reach a wider market through TSP domestic coverage.
